About

The Rasrotor project is the brainchild of Udit Gupta and Michael Whittaker. What exactly is the Rasrotor? It will be a combination of a quadrotor and a Raspberry Pi. What exactly will the Rasrotor accomplish? Well, Udit and Michael aren't really sure yet. The Rasrotor project is as much an application of what they know as it is an exploration into what they don't. Far from well versed in fields such as web design or networking, Udit and Michael are learning the ropes as they go along. Without a clear idea of what's ahead, all they can do is hope for the best and learn a lot along the way.

If you have any ideas, advice, or words of wisdom for these two brave souls, feel free to send them an email. Any and all input is welcome!
